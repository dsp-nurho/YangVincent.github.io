About us

Our History

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities; to encourage scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice; to promote closer affiliation between the commercial world and students of commerce, and to further a higher standard of commercial ethics and culture and the civic and commercial welfare of the community.

  • Founders - Alexander Frank Makay, Alfred Moysello, Henry Albert Tienken, Harold Valentine Jacobs
  • Nu Rho - Founded on December 3rd, 1994 at UC Davis
  • Network - 287 established collegiate chapters
  • Brothers - Over 260,000 initiated Brothers

Presidential Statement

As America's premier business fraternity, Delta Sigma Pi is committed to the ideals of leadership, service, and scholarship. The Nu Rho Chapter offers UC Davis students an exceptional opportunity to gain practical business experience, expand their professional network, and experience the lifelong bond of brotherhood.

Our diverse group of alumni have gone on to become leaders in all fields of business. Investment bankers, consultants, entrepreneurs and executives alike, we have all benefitted from the professional guidance of Delta Sigma Pi. For an ambitious individual seeking to make a difference and achieve in the professional world, there is no better platform.

Beyond its focus on professional excellence, our fraternity also offers the social experiences and peer support system that make college memorable. Our members are among the most active students on campus and there is no group of more intelligent, friendly, and fun people. I highly encourage you to attend our upcoming recruitment events and learn more about becoming a part of our brotherhood

Jin Zhang, President | Delta Sigma Pi -- Nu Rho Chapter
